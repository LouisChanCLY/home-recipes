## Ingredients
- Sea bass — 392.2g (127 kcal/100g)
- Sweet potato — 402.7g
- Okra — 153g
- Cherry tomatoes (Vittoria) — 138g
- Red onion — 98g
- Olive oil (1st) — 17.5g (842 kcal/100ml)
- Olive oil (2nd) — 8g
- Yuzu dressing — 12g (334 kcal/100ml)
- Rice (raw) — ~165g total (~55g per portion)

## Method Notes
Okra: trim only the stem end (hard woody cap). Leave the tail — it crisps up nicely. Halve lengthways.
Roast veg in oven first, add sea bass partway through.
Yuzu dressing drizzled at the end — don't cook it in.

## Nutrition Notes
Per portion (÷3): ~600 kcal target.
- Veg + fish: ~413 kcal
- Rice (raw ~55g per portion): ~190 kcal
- Total: ~600 kcal

## Ideas & Variations
- No mushrooms in this version
- More yuzu dressing on the side if needed
- Works well as a lighter dinner
