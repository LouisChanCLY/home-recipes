## Ingredients
- Chicken wings — 440g total (147g per portion)
- Sweet potato — 348g total (116g per portion)
- Carrots — 335.5g total (112g per portion)
- Onion — 191g total (64g per portion)
- Extra virgin olive oil — 23.6g total (7.9g per portion)
- Yuzu dressing — 33g total (11g per portion)
- Garlic — several cloves, unpeeled
- Fresh rosemary
- Salt, pepper

## Method Notes
Pat wings dry, season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, smoked paprika. Veg in similar-sized chunks, tossed in EVOO, salt, pepper, rosemary tucked in. Veg goes in at 200°C for 15 mins first, then crank to 220°C, add wings, roast together 35–40 mins flipping halfway. Finish with yuzu dressing.

## Ideas & Variations
Yuzu dressing works well as a finishing drizzle — adds brightness against the roasted sweetness of the veg.

## Pairings
Works as a standalone one-tray meal.
