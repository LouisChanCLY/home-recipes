## Ingredients
- Sweet potato — 402.7g
- Okra — 153g
- Cherry tomatoes (Vittoria) — 138g
- Red onion — 98g
- Olive oil (roasting) — 17.5g (842 kcal/100ml)
- Olive oil (second addition) — 8g
- Yuzu dressing — 12g (334 kcal/100ml)

## Method Notes
Okra: trim only the stem end (hard woody cap). Leave the tail — it crisps up nicely. Halve lengthways.
Roast sweet potato and red onion first as they take longer.
Add okra and cherry tomatoes partway through.
Yuzu dressing drizzled over at the end — don't roast it in.

## Nutrition Notes
Per portion (÷3): ~247 kcal including oil and dressing.

## Ideas & Variations
- No mushrooms in this version
- More yuzu dressing on the side
- Works as a standalone side or with any simply cooked fish

## Pairings
- Oven Baked Sea Bass
- Rice
