## Ingredients
- Skinless chicken thighs — 500g
- Sweet potato — 513g, 2cm chunks
- Carrot — 351g, thick batons
- Courgette — 310g, half-moons
- Onion — 155g, wedges
- Sainsbury's light pesto — 60g
- Olive oil — 15g
- Yuzu dressing — 15g
- Pine nuts — 10g, toasted

## Method Notes
Preheat oven 200C fan. Toss all veg and chicken separately in pesto, olive oil, yuzu dressing, garlic, lemon juice, salt and cracked black pepper. Sweet potato and carrot on one tray — roast 20 mins first. Chicken and onion on a second tray — into oven at same time. At 20 min mark, flip everything, add courgette to the veg tray. Another 20–25 mins until chicken is golden and cooked through. Rest chicken 5 mins. Toast pine nuts in a dry pan, scatter over before serving.

## Ideas & Variations
- Cherry tomatoes (150g) into veg tray last 15 mins — burst and create a natural pesto sauce
- Crumble feta over at the end for salty contrast
- Chilli flakes in the marinade for background heat
- Balsamic glaze drizzle over plated dish
- Diced sourdough croutons tossed in last 10 mins to soak pesto juices
- Cashews as alternative to pine nuts — add raw last 20 mins

## Pairings
Serve as a complete one-tray meal. Works with a simple green salad on the side.
