## Method Notes
- Roast in oven alongside the vegetables.
- Sea bass added partway through roasting so it doesn't overcook.

## Ideas & Variations
- Yuzu dressing drizzled over at the end
- Lemon and herbs work well
- Served alongside roasted sweet potato, okra, and cherry tomatoes

## Pairings
- Roasted Sweet Potato, Okra & Cherry Tomatoes
- Rice
