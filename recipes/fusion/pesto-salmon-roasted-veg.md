## Ingredients
- Stamford Street skin-on salmon fillets — 480g total (160g per portion)
- Sweet potato (2cm cubes) — 330g
- Red onion (wedges) — 195g
- Courgette (sliced, salted first) — 190g
- Chestnut mushrooms — 110g
- Sainsbury's So Organic cherry tomatoes on the vine — 78.5g
- Sainsbury's Lighter Green Pesto — 40g
- Olive oil — 20g
- Yuzu dressing (叶匠寿庵 French yuzu) — half tablespoon
- Lemon zest and juice — 1 lemon

## Method Notes
Oven at 210°C fan.

T+0 — Sweet potato tossed in olive oil, smoked paprika, salt. Spread on tray. Roast 35–40 min.

T+15 — Mushrooms on separate tray, light oil, high heat, don't crowd. Red onion wedges and courgette (salted and patted dry) on another tray with cherry tomatoes. 20–25 min.

T+20 — Pat salmon dry. Mix pesto with lemon zest and press firmly on top of each fillet, skin side down on lightly oiled tray. Roast 15–18 min, pull at 52–55°C internal.

Finish — Squeeze lemon over veg tray straight out of oven. Drizzle yuzu dressing over veg. Serve immediately.

## Ideas & Variations
- Add panko to pesto mixture for extra crust
- Swap courgette for asparagus in season
- Try with bell peppers for added sweetness and colour

## Pairings
- The yuzu dressing ties the veg tray together — don't skip it
- A light white wine (Sauvignon Blanc or Pinot Grigio) would work well alongside
