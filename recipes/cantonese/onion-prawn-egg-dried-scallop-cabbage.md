## Ingredients

### 洋蔥蝦仁炒蛋
- Frozen peeled raw prawns — 106g
- Sainsbury's large eggs — 8
- Onion — 175.5g
- Soy sauce (marinade) — 2.5g
- Sesame oil (marinade) — 2g
- Cooking oil — ~12g (estimated)

### 瑤柱浸黃芽白
- Dried scallops (瑤柱) — 6.5g
- Chinese leaf (黃芽白) — 314g
- 粉絲 (glass noodles) — 70g
- Oyster sauce — 13g
- Cooking oil — ~5g (estimated)

### Rice
- Rice (raw) — 210g (70g per portion)

## Method Notes

**炒蛋:** Marinate prawns in soy and sesame oil. Wok hei essential — high heat, fast. Beat eggs well. Cook prawns first until just pink, remove, return with eggs and toss. Don't overcook.

**浸黃芽白:** Soak 瑤柱 in warm water 30 mins, shred, keep soaking liquid — liquid gold for the braise. Braise Chinese leaf gently in the 瑤柱 broth with oyster sauce. Add 粉絲 to soak up the broth. No onion in this dish — keep it clean.

Onion stays in the 炒蛋 only.

## Ideas & Variations
- Splash of Shaoxing wine when prawns hit the wok for 鑊氣
- White pepper and dash of fish sauce in the egg mixture for depth
- Spring onion cut into 寸段 tossed in right at the end
- Goji berries (杞子) in the 浸黃芽白 for sweetness and colour
- Ginger slices in the braise
