## Ingredients
- Chicken thigh (skinless) — 418g total (~209g per portion)
- Homemade teriyaki sauce: soy sauce, dark soy sauce, mirin, cooking wine, sweetener
- Onions — 187g (~94g per portion)
- Lettuce — stir-fried with garlic, oil, oyster sauce (side dish, not saved separately)

## Sauce Quantities
- Soy sauce — 30g
- Dark soy sauce — 3.5g
- Mirin — 30g
- Cooking wine — 25g
- Sweetener (代糖) — to taste

## Method Notes
Mix sauce cold. Sear chicken thighs, pour sauce over, reduce until glossy and chicken is cooked through.
Onions sliced into half-moons, cooked down in pan before adding chicken — absorbs the sauce well.
Serve with steamed rice for Louis, rice-free for Kelly.

## Ideas & Variations
- Add shimeji or shiitake mushrooms — soaks up the sauce nicely
- Spring onions as garnish at the end

## Pairings
- Stir-fried lettuce with garlic and oyster sauce
