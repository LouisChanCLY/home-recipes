## Ingredients
- Sainsbury's Prosciutto Cappelletti — 1/2 pack (150g)
- Butter — 1.5 tsp (7g)
- Garlic — 1 clove
- Fresh thyme — small handful

## Method Notes
- Garlic and thyme sweated low in butter on gentle heat — no browning, just fragrance.
- Pasta cooked per pack instructions. Splash of pasta water reserved before draining.
- Tossed pasta through the butter off heat with pasta water to emulsify into a light sauce.
- Classic Northern Italian — no cream, no cheese, let the prosciutto filling do the work.

## Ideas & Variations
- Could try sage instead of thyme for a more traditional burro e salvia approach
- A grating of Parmigiano at the end wouldn't be wrong if you want more depth
- Try the full pack next time for 2 portions — scales cleanly

## Pairings
- A light, dry white would work well — something like a Pinot Grigio or Soave
- Simple bitter leaf salad on the side (radicchio, endive) to cut through the butter
- Would pair well with a small glass of sparkling water with lemon during cooking
