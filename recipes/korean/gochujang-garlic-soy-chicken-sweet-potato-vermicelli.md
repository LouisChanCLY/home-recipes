## Ingredients

### Batter
- Chicken thighs (boneless, skinless) — 500g
- Gochujang — 11g
- Soy sauce — 5ml
- Sesame oil — 8ml
- Erythritol or monk fruit sweetener — 6g
- White pepper — 1g
- Rice vinegar — 15ml
- Cornstarch — 36g
- Cold water — 30-40ml to loosen

### Garlic Soy Finishing Sauce
- Neutral oil — 15ml
- Garlic — 4 cloves, grated
- Soy sauce — 22ml
- Sesame oil — 8ml
- Water — 15ml

### Sweet Potato Vermicelli (紅薯粉絲)
- Sweet potato vermicelli dry — 138.4g
- Soy sauce — 10ml
- Sesame oil — 5ml
- Spring onions — 3, thinly sliced

### Quick Pickled Carrots
- Carrots, julienned — 143.5g
- Salt — 2g
- Rice vinegar — 20ml
- Sweetener — 3g

### Veg Bed (doubles as rack)
- Mushrooms — 128g, torn into chunks
- Courgette — 188g, sliced into 1cm half-moons

## Method Notes

**Batter:** Whisk gochujang, soy, sesame oil, sweetener, white pepper, rice vinegar and cornstarch together until smooth. Add cold water gradually until ribbon consistency — coats back of spoon and drips slowly. Pat chicken completely dry, score 2-3 times, coat in batter, let excess drip off, straight onto veg bed in preheated oven.

**Veg bed:** Toss mushrooms and courgette in a little oil and soy. Spread on foil-lined tray. Chicken goes directly on top — veg acts as the rack, keeps chicken off the tray, absorbs drippings.

**Oven:** 220°C fan. Top third of oven. 15 mins, flip carefully, 10-12 mins more. Grill/broil final 3-4 mins to blister and crisp. Internal temp 75°C. Rest 5 mins before slicing.

**Finishing sauce:** While chicken rests, low heat pan, oil in, grated garlic, sweat 60-90 secs no colour. Add soy + water, stir 30 secs. Off heat, add sesame oil last. Toss hot chicken in sauce before slicing.

**Pickled carrots:** Julienne thin, toss with salt, rice vinegar, sweetener. 20-30 mins at room temp, drain. Serve cold alongside noodles.

**Noodles:** Soak in just-boiled water 5-6 mins until translucent throughout. Drain well. Toss immediately with soy, sesame oil, spring onions while hot. For lunchbox dress lighter — flavour intensifies overnight.

**Lunchbox pack:** Noodles at bottom, sliced chicken on top, veg to one side, pickled carrot alongside. Gochujang from chicken seeps into noodles by lunchtime.

## Key Tips
- Pat chicken completely dry before battering — non-negotiable
- Batter to oven in under 2 mins — don't let it sit or it goes gummy
- Never cook sesame oil — always add off heat
- Grill last 3-4 mins is what gives you the crust — don't skip
- Mushrooms shrink massively — keep pieces large
